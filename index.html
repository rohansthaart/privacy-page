Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Welcome to Calc Master. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

Calc Master does NOT collect any personally identifiable information.

  • No account registration required
  • No name, email, phone number, or address collected
  • No payment or card information stored

Usage Data

We do not track or store user activity, calculation history, or behavior analytics on any server. All calculations and history remain locally on your device.

App Permissions

Calc Master may request minimal permissions only for functionality purposes such as:

  • Haptic feedback (vibration)
  • Basic device features required by Android system

These permissions are used strictly to improve user experience and are never used to collect personal data.

Data Storage

Any locally saved preferences or calculation history are stored only on your device. We do not upload, sync, or back up your data to external servers.

Third-Party Services

Calc Master does not use third-party services that collect personal user data.

Children's Privacy

Calc Master does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected, the app is safe for general use.

Security

We value your trust and strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting your information. Since Calc Master does not transmit or store personal data online, your privacy risk is minimal.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. You are advised to review this page periodically for updates.
